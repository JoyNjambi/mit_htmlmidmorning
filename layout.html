KENYAN LEVELS OF EDUCATION

The current kenyan education system is 8-4-4, meaning that a student has to go through 8 years of primary school studies, then four of high school and finally four years at the university..........this is ofcourse after pertaking in the less vigorous basic nursery school system that seeks to ensure that a learner knows how to read and write.
